Gameplay balance update — fixes #3

March 20, 2026

General changes to investments and growth: life, spirit, neutral resistances, item ability range, plus item balancing and hero-specific changes — including Street Brawl mode.

General

  • Vitality investment tree bonus reverted back to % base HP increase rather than raw constant bonus (back to how it was set up in December).
  • Hero health growth increased by +3 and 4%.
  • Spirit investment rescaled from 7/11/15/19/38/52/64/76/89/101 to 7/11/15/19/38/48/57/66/75/100.
  • Small neutrals spirit resist increased from 35% to 45%.

Ability range — item modifiers

  • Greater Expansion: Ability Range reduced from 35% to 30%.
  • Guardian Ward: Ability Range reduced from 10% to 8%.
  • Ballistic Enchantment: Ability Range reduced from 25% to 20%.
  • Cultist Sacrifice: Ability Range reduced from +15% to +12%.
  • Healing Nova: Ability Range reduced from +6% to +5%.
  • Rescue Beam: Ability Range reduced from +8% to +6%.
  • Knockdown: Ability Range reduced from +6% to +5%.
  • Divine Barrier: Ability Range reduced from +15% to +10%.
  • Arcane Surge: Ability Range reduced from 15% to 12%.
  • Echo Shard: No longer grants +5% Ability Range.
  • Spirit Burn: Ability Range reduced from +8% to +6%.
  • Vortex Web: Ability Range reduced from +10% to +8%.

Item balance

  • Weighted Shots: Debuff Resist increased from 18% to 20%.
  • Burst Fire: Duration increased from 3.5s to 4.5s.
  • Burst Fire: Cooldown increased from 7s to 9s.
  • Blood Tribute: Spirit Resist reduced from 10% to 8%.
  • Enchanter's Emblem: Spirit Resist increased from 15% to 18%.
  • Restorative Locket: Spirit Resist increased from 8% to 10%.
  • Metal Skin: Bullet Resist reduced from 15% to 12%.
  • Counterspell: Bonus Health reduced from 100 to 75.
  • Golden Goose Egg: Now has a 2 second channel to hatch.
  • Boundless Spirit: Constant spirit bonus reduced from 35 to 30.
